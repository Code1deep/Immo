Investor Value & Trust (Financial Growth and ESG)

Ensuring transparency, confidence, and financial attractiveness for impact investors.

A. SIH Sustainable Yield Fund

A dedicated green investment fund enabling investors to directly participate in sustainable growth (seeds, bio, training). Goal: Attract rapid capital inflow while reinforcing transparency and credibility.

B. ESG Dashboard (Public)

Monitors biodiversity rate, green energy usage, and rural job creation. Result: Strengthened trust from institutional investors and green funds.

C. “SIH Impact” Label & Blockchain Traceability

A certified internal label guaranteeing local origin, sustainability, and social responsibility. Blockchain-based traceability ensures end-to-end transparency for every product journey.
