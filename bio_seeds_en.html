Main Mission

Collect, protect, and reproduce ancient seed varieties adapted to local climates, ensuring biodiversity and food sovereignty.

Strategic Goals

  • Recover ancient wheat, barley, and corn varieties from regional collections.
  • Preserve genetic diversity by establishing a living seed bank.
  • Support farmers in certified organic multiplication.

Projects in Progress

Creation of regional seed gardens and partnerships with agricultural institutes for the preservation of ancient genetic lines.
