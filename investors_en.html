Transparency, Legality and Security

Each investment is backed by a land title legally registered and certified by the competent local authorities. A sworn notary and an independent law firm validate every land or real estate acquisition. You thus receive the official, legally certified proof that your capital is converted into a real and secured asset.

  • 📜 Land Guarantee: each project corresponds to a sworn, verified, and notarized land title.
  • 📊 Quarterly performance reports available in your investor dashboard.
  • 🔒 Annual audit of funds and assets by an independent audit firm.
  • 🌱 ESG Commitment: all our projects promote food and energy sustainability.

Our Financial Model

In accordance with our status as a Hybrid Investment Company (SIH), 90% of the Funds From Operations (FFO) are distributed quarterly to our investors. This model ensures financial flow stability and full transparency of returns.

💰 Allocation of our assets:

  • 65% Commercial real estate (offices, category A retail)
  • 35% Prime agricultural land under long-term leases
