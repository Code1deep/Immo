💰 FAQ – Common Investor Questions

Q1. How is the dividend yield calculated?

As a FII, we distribute at least 90% of Funds From Operations (FFO), i.e., rental income before amortization. This ensures high transparency and consistent cash flow.

Q2. Is the return guaranteed?

No. Returns depend on rental performance. The 13–15% targets are based on current lease projections.

Q3. How do we minimize risk?

By diversifying between commercial real estate (urban growth) and farmland (defensive, inflation-resistant assets).
